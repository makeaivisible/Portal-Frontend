# Contributing

Thank you for helping Make AI Visible.

## Working Norms

- Treat privacy as a product requirement, not a later review step.
- Use synthetic or anonymized data in examples, tests, screenshots, and issues.
- Keep pull requests focused and explain the privacy impact of the change.
- Mark legal, IRB, security, or expert-validation assumptions clearly.

## Pull Request Checklist

- The change is scoped to the repository's responsibility.
- Tests, fixtures, or documentation were added where useful.
- No raw contributor conversations, secrets, or personal data were committed.
- New public outputs avoid individual-level records and small cohorts.
